crowdhealth.io

Crowd sourced health service information for LGBTQ* people

The Project

crowdhealth.io is a project concieved by Camille and Rachel at a NHS Hack Day in November 2013. When looking for health services that met our individual needs we were often unable to find the information we were looking for.

The project has undergone the following stages:

  • Rapid prototype at NHS Hack Day
  • Information Architecture (in-progress)
  • Brochure Website & Project Name Hack Day
