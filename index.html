The Project

crowdhealth.io is a project concieved by Camille and Rachel at a NHS Hack Day in November 2013. When looking for health services that met our individual needs we were often unable to find the information we were looking for.

We created three personas, Nicola, Sunil and Caroline and attempted to find information that they would look for on nhs.uk. In most cases the information was hard to find and not local. In others it was missing, or presented poorly for the audience.
